Directions

You can travel to Findhorn by air, train, bus and car. A one-way system operates in Findhorn; follow the road along the bay (passing the Kimberley Inn), then follow the road down the side of the Crown and Anchor Inn and take the first turning on the left (at the Bunty Fish and Chip Shop). Culbin Sands apartments are on the right hand side.

From the airport

  1. There are regular flights to Inverness and Aberdeen airport from many UK and international airports (including easyJet, Ryanair, KLM UK, British Airways, bmi, Loganair, Eastern Airways and Air France). Inverness airport is approximately 30 miles from Findhorn and Aberdeen airport is about 75 miles away by road.

  2. From Inverness airport, take the Jet 11 to Forres, getting off at St Leonards Church. You can then take the number 34 bus to Findhorn or hire a taxi. Alternatively, you can take a taxi from the airport to Findhorn for around £40.

  3. From Aberdeen airport, take the number 80 bus to Dyce railway startion where you can connect to the train service which runs from Aberdeen to Invernesss and stops at Forres train station. You can also take the Jet 727 bus to Aberdeen city and then change onto the number 10 bus to Forres. At Forres, you can take the number 34 bus to Findhorn or take a taxi.

From the train station

  1. The local train station is Forres. There are good rail connections from Glasgow, Edinburgh and Aberdeen. There are sleeper trains from London to Inverness and Aberdeen, with connections to Forres.

  2. Public buses run from Forres Station to the town centre, and then from there to Findhorn. You can take the 34 bus from Forres to Findhorn which takes about 20 minutes (please note that there is not a Sunday service of this bus).

  3. For details of train tickets look at National Rail Enquiries, The Trainline and East Coast.

By car

  1. Findhorn is signposted on the Forres by-pass and you can take two routes by road, either;

  2. Take the A9 from Perth to Inverness and then taking the A96 to Forres.

  3. Take the A90 from Perth to Aberdeen and then take the A96 to Forres.

Location

Located on the coast of the Moray Firth in Scotland, Findhorn is a traditional Scottish seaside village hosting views of the dramatic Scottish coast. Findhorn is also home to the Findhorn Foundation; a spiritual community, ecovillage and an international centre for holistic learning.
