Location

Located on the coast of the Moray Firth in Scotland, Findhorn is a traditional Scottish seaside village hosting views of the dramatic Scottish coast. Findhorn is also home to the Findhorn Foundation; a spiritual community, ecovillage and an international centre for holistic learning.
