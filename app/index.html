A haven of peace and tranquility

Findhorn is a haven of peace and tranquillity. Culbin Sands is an extremely attractive ground floor flat set in a turret, with wonderful views over the dunes to the Moray Firth. This spacious and comfortable apartment is available for holiday accommodation in tranquil seaside location on the Moray coast, Scotland. Sleeps five adults and a baby.

Accomodation Details

 

Living Room

The spacious living room is nestled in the turret of the apartment building providing views of the surrounding dunes. There is ample seating, a dining table, TV, DVD and free Wi-Fi.

 

Kitchen

The modern kitchen is fully equipped with a fridge freezer, electric cooker, microwave, washing machine and dryer.

 

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom has a comfortable king size double bed and has an en-suite shower, toilet and wash hand basin.

 

Twin Bedroom

The twin bedroom has beautiful views across the dunes and comfortably sleeps three with two beds and a foldaway bed. There is also a cot available for a baby.

Location

Located on the coast of the Moray Firth in Scotland, Findhorn is a traditional Scottish seaside village hosting views of the dramatic Scottish coast. Findhorn is also home to the Findhorn Foundation; a spiritual community, ecovillage and an international centre for holistic learning.
